# Credits & Data Attribution

## Data Sources

### International Football Results — martj42/international_results
- **Source:** [github.com/martj42/international_results](https://github.com/martj42/international_results)
- **License:** [CC0 1.0 Universal (Public Domain Dedication)](https://creativecommons.org/publicdomain/zero/1.0/)
- **Files used:** `results.csv`, `shootouts.csv`, `former_names.csv`
- **Description:** Historical international football results from 1872 to present, including
  match outcomes, shootout results, and team name change history.

### World Bank World Development Indicators (WDI)
- **Source:** [World Bank Open Data](https://data.worldbank.org)
- **License:** [Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 (CC BY 4.0)](https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by/4.0/)
- **Indicators used:**
  - `SP.POP.TOTL` — Population, total
  - `NY.GDP.MKTP.CD` — GDP, current US$
  - `NY.GDP.PCAP.CD` — GDP per capita, current US$
- **Attribution:** Data sourced from The World Bank, World Development Indicators.
  Per CC BY 4.0 terms, this attribution is required wherever derived data or outputs
  are displayed publicly, including on the results website at
  [world-cup-simulation.lalutir.com](https://world-cup-simulation.lalutir.com).
